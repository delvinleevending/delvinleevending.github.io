Donations

Pay It Forward's mission is to fight against food insecurity issues for college students at UCSD by providing students with access to nutritious and delicious hot meals for free. All donations will go to the Food Cooperative @ The Old Student Center. The Food Cooperative will then use the money to buy ingredients and equipment to provide students with free meals. The CEO has volunteered his time at the Food Cooperative as one of the cooks for over 2 years now and he can assure you that all the money raised will go towards bettering the space and towards buying ingredients to make food for hungry college students. Check out the Food Coop's Website, Instagram, and Facebook.
